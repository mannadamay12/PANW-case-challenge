TypographyReading is the core activity. We separate "Interface" from "Content".UI Font (Headings, Buttons, Nav): Inter or Plus Jakarta SansWhy: Highly legible at small sizes, neutral character.Journal Font (Entries, Editor): Merriweather or LoraWhy: Serif fonts reduce eye strain for long-form reading and feel more "personal."3.
